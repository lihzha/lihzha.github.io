Lihan Zha

I am a senior undergraduate student at Weiyang College in Tsinghua University, double majoring in Mathematics and Physics and Energy and Power Engineering. I'm interested in foundation models for robotics, human-robot interaction and data-driven methods for robotics and control. My research goal is to enable robots to interact intelligently and safely with the world.

Currently I'm an undergraduate visiting researcher at Stanford Intelligent and Interactive Autonomous Systems Group (ILIAD), advised by Prof. Dorsa Sadigh, where I work on enabling robots to respond and distill generalizable knowledge from humans' corrections. Prior to that, I worked with Prof. Jianyu Chen on reinforcement learning for complex dynamics. I was also very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dongxiang Jiang, where I worked on time series analysis.
